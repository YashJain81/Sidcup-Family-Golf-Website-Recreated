ABOUT US

Home to a 46-bay, multi-tier, floodlit driving range, powered by Toptracer Range technology. Complimented by a practice green and bunker, coffee shop and American Golf Store. There truly is something for everyone as we also boast two outdoor 18-hole dinosaur themed crazy golf courses.
Please note: we are a cashless venue. The range closes at 10pm with last balls at 9pm.

TOPRACER RANGE




Experience precision golf practice with our state-of-the-art TopRacer Range, offering immersive virtual golf simulations for players of all levels.

ADVENTURE GOLF




Embark on a fun-filled golfing adventure with our themed mini-golf courses, perfect for family outings and group entertainment.

GOLF LESSONS




Elevate your game with personalized golf instruction from our certified professionals, tailored to enhance your skills and boost your confidence on the course.
